Our Impact

Every event becomes a gallery of confidence, connection, and color.

Drawn Together grows through hands-on classes and the community partners who help make space for children with neurological conditions to create freely.

30+ families reached through inclusive art experiences
5+ partnership organizations supporting the mission
100% focused on accessible arts and crafts

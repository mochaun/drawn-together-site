Our Mission

Building belonging one project at a time.

At Drawn Together, we aim to create an inclusive program that empowers children of all abilities to express themselves, connect with others, and celebrate their unique creativity.

Our classes are centered around supporting kids with neurological conditions through adaptable, friendly art activities led by volunteers who care about confidence, community, and joy.

Accessible Projects

Crafts are designed to be approachable, flexible, and fun for different abilities and comfort levels.

What We Offer

One welcoming arts and crafts class format.

Instead of multiple program tracks, we focus on one strong experience: inclusive arts and crafts sessions for kids with neurological conditions.

Inclusive Arts & Crafts Class

Each session includes a guided craft, flexible pacing, adaptive support, and time for kids to create side by side. Projects may include seasonal crafts, sensory-friendly art, take-home keepsakes, and collaborative pieces.

  • Free for participating children
  • Designed for kids with neurological conditions
  • Hosted with community partner spaces when available
